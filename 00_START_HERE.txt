## 🎓 Quick Reference

### Supported Formats
✅ MP4 (Best)
✅ WebM
✅ Ogg

### Requirements
- Modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- 50MB+ free storage
- Video file in MP4 format

### Tips
- Max 500MB per video
- Compress large files (use HandBrake)
- Test playback before using
- Backup your videos
