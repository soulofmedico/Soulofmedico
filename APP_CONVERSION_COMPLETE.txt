STEP 1: Open the App
1. Double-click login.html or open it in a web browser
2. (OR) Open file path: c:\Users\Admin\OneDrive\Desktop\SOM APP\login.html
3. Wait for page to fully load (5-10 seconds)

STEP 2: Install on Device

FOR ANDROID:
- Look for "Install app" button (usually appears automatically)
- Or tap menu (⋮) → "Install app" or "Add to home screen"
- Tap "Install"
- App appears on your home screen

FOR iPHONE/iPad:
- Tap Share (↑) at bottom
- Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
- Tap "Add"
- App appears on your home screen

FOR WINDOWS/MAC:
- Look for Install icon (↓) in address bar
- Or click menu (⋮) → "Install Soul of Medico"
- App opens in separate window
- Desktop shortcut created

STEP 3: Start Using!
- Login with default credentials (see below)
- Explore all features offline
- Upload videos via Admin Panel
- Watch videos anytime, anywhere

❓ FAQ
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: Do I need internet to use the app?
A: No! After the first load (when online), everything is cached. Works offline.

Q: How many videos can I upload?
A: Depends on device storage. Most devices: 50-500MB total app storage.

Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?
A: Yes! Each device gets its own copy. Videos are stored locally on each.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! Data stays on your device. Nothing sent to servers.

Q: Can I share videos with others?
A: Only if they have access to your device. For sharing, you'd need a backend server.

Q: What if I uninstall the app?
A: Videos will be deleted from that device. Install again to get a fresh app.

Q: Why no YouTube videos?
A: YouTube was blocked/causing errors. Local videos work much better!

Q: How do I update the app?
A: Service worker auto-updates. Next launch checks for new version.
