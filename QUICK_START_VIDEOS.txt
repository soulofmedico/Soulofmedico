# 🎬 QUICK START: ADD VIDEOS IN 5 STEPS

## ⚡ Super Quick Guide

### 1. Go to Lectures Page
Click **Lectures** from home screen

### 2. Click "+ Add Video"
Blue button in top-right corner

### 3. Fill in Details
- Title: "Your Lecture Name"
- Subject: Select from dropdown
- Duration: e.g., "45 min"
- Upload: Select video file from computer

### 4. Add Description & Notes
Write what the video is about

### 5. Click "✅ Save Lecture"
Done! Video is saved

---

## ✅ Supported Video Formats
- **MP4** ✅ (Best)
- **WebM** ✅
- **Ogg** ✅

---

## 📝 Example

```
Title:       IUPAC Nomenclature
Subject:     Chemistry
Duration:    45 min
Video File:  chemistry-iupac.mp4
Description: Learn how to name organic compounds
Notes:       Important for organic chemistry
```

---

## 🎮 Once Video is Added

Click **"Watch"** to play
- Play/Pause: Click video
- Speed: 1x, 1.5x, 2x (bottom)
- Full Screen: Double-click
- Description: View lecture info

---

## 🆘 Problems?

### "File too large?"
Compress video first (HandBrake app)
Max: 500MB

### "Video doesn't play?"
Convert to MP4 format first

### "Lost my videos?"
Check HOW_TO_ADD_VIDEOS.md for backup
